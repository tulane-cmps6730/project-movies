CLAS 4050Intro to Field Arch: Ashkelon(3)This is an interdisciplinary course of archaeological fieldwork based in experiential learning at a complex, multi-period Tell site on the southern Levantine coast.  Students work five full days plus one half-day in the field uncovering artifacts and learning archaeological field techniques.
Corequisite(s):CLAS 3050.Corequisite(s):CLAS 3050.
