GLSP 4160Interviewing & Investigation(3)Through lectures and practical exercises, students learn principles, methods, techniques for identifying, obtaining, documenting, and disseminating information in civil and criminal actions; and develop the interviewing skills necessary to communicate effectively while avoiding ethical pitfalls.
Pre-requisites:GLSP 3030,3060and3070, or approval of Director.Prerequisite(s):GLSP 3030,3060and3070.
