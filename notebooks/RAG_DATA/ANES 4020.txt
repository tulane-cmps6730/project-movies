ANES 4020Basics of Anesthesiology(2)This course is an introductory course to the practice of Anesthesiology. Students participating in this rotation will be introduced to the most common anesthesia subspecialties including General Anesthesia, Obstetric Anesthesia, Regional Anesthesia, and the Preoperative evaluation process.  Course may be repeated up to unlimited credit hours.Maximum Hours:99
