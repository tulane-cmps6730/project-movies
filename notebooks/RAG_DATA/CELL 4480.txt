CELL 4480Head and Neck Anatomy(3)This 3 credit lecture and laboratory course focuses on the gross anatomy of the head and neck, as well as central nervous system anatomy. This includes cadaver dissection of the head, neck, cranial nerves, and brain. This class mirrors dental and medical school anatomy courses and will prepare students to succeed in medical school, dental school, or allied health professions.  Does not count toward CMB major.

Prerequisite:CELL 4490,6490.Prerequisite(s):CELL 4490or6490.
