SWHL 1020Swahili II(3)Elementary Swahili II is a second level introductory course for beginners of Swahili language. This course is open to students who have taken and passed SHWL I010. In this course students will further develop the four language skills: listening, speaking, reading and writing.  Pre-requisites: Elementary Swahili I (SWHL 1010) or instructor's permission.Prerequisite(s):SWHL 1010.
