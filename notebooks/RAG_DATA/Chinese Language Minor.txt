Chinese Language Minor Overview Requirements Students selecting a minor in Chinese Language must complete five Chinese language courses. Chinese language courses used to fulfill the NTC undergraduate language proficiency requirement may not be counted toward this minor. Incoming students who are placed in ASTC 2040 Intermediate Chinese II (4 c.h.) and who are planning to pursue a minor in Chinese language are encouraged to take a proficiency test before the fall semester starts. Students selecting a minor in Chinese Language must complete five Chinese language courses.
