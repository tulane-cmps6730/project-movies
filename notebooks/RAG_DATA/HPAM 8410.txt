HPAM 8410Cost Benefit/Effective Analysi(3)The purpose of the course is to introduce techniques of economic evaluation applied to health interventions and clinical decision making.  Topics covered include:  cost analysis, effectiveness measures, cost-effectiveness, utility measures and cost-utility analysis, benefits of health interventions and cost-benefit analysis.  The course will discuss a number of case studies in clinical health economics to illustrate the use of economic evaluation techniques in the health sector.  There are no prerequisites for the course but eh student should be familiar with basic geometry, algebra and statistics.
