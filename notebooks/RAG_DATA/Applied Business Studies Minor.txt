Applied Business Studies Minor Overview Requirements The 18-credit Minor in Applied Business Studies allows students to enhance their writing skills, develop business ethics, and gain practical knowledge in key areas of business such as accounting, management, and Human Resources.  Students can also explore marketing, finance, economics, or the legal aspects of business as part of this minor. * Catalog Addenda Note: Minor curricular adjustments were made to this minor in Spring 2024.
