BMEN 6010Physical Dimen of Aging(3)This course is designed to introduce students to the physiological, behavioral, and socio-economic changes associated with aging. In particular, we will focus on the effects of exercise on the aging human system. We will also discuss what it means to become older within a community, what can a person expect during the aging process, and what kind of control a person has over his/her aging body.

Prerequisite(s):CELL 1010orEBIO 1010.Prerequisite(s):CELL 1010orEBIO 1010.
