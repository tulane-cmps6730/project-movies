SPAN 3080Spanish Grammar and Writing for the Legal Professions(3)This course offers students the opportunity to enhance existing Spanish communication skills in legal practice. Students will also improve writing skills through assignments to be completed outside of class. The course will introduce Spanish legal terminology in areas such as immigration, consumer protection, criminal, employment, housing and family law.Prerequisite(s):SPAN 2040, minimum score of PASS in 'SPAN 3040Placement', minimum score of PASS in 'SPAN 3050Placement' or minimum score of PASS in 'SPAN 3060Placement'.
