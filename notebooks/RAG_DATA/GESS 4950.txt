GESS 4950Gender & Sexuality Theory II(3,4)This course is the second in a sequence of two courses on feminist and queer theory. The primary goals of this course are 1) to map the expansion of feminist and queer theory in recent decades, 2) critically engage with theories of gender, sexuality, race, and class to gain understanding of inequality and social change.
Prerequisite(s):GESS 4940*.
