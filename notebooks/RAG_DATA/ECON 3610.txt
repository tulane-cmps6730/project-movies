ECON 3610Games & Strategic Behavior(3)This course provides an introduction to game theory -- the formal study of strategic situations -- and its applications.  The course will cover the basic analysis of simultaneous and sequential move games with perfect and imperfect information.  This material will be followed by a number of applications which illustrate how the use of game theory can help us to improve our understanding of strategic behavior in economic, political, and social situations.
