DDSN 2100Intro to UX Design(3)This studio course explores the field of user-experience design, empathy, user-engagement and best practices. Students will explore and evaluate case studies, create concept-driven strategies to meet the needs to of mock-clients and produce wireframes and prototypes. Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1101,1102.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1101and1102.
