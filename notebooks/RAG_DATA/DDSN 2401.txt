DDSN 2401Design Studio I(3)This studio course explores development of printed branding materials within the Adobe interface. Students will create concept-driven projects exploring execution via various media to communicate an event, promotion or series. Students will work with mock clients to create professional written and verbal presentations for portfolio-ready printed works.
Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401,2100,2400.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 2100and2400.
