LATN 2030Intro to Latin Literature(4)This course provides an introduction to Latin prose and poetry through readings from some of the great works of Latin literature.  While reading a broad selection of Latin texts, we will consider how Roman literature evolved along with the changing culture and politics of the city.  We will also learn about important authors, historical figures, and events that you are likely to encounter again later in your studies.  The class emphasizes precise and accurate translation, vocabulary building, and grammatical understanding. Prerequisite:LATN 1020or equivalent.Prerequisite(s):minimum score of PASS in 'LATN 2030Placement' orLATN 1020.
