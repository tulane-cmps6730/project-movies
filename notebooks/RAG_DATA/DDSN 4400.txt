DDSN 4400Business of Design(3)This studio course provides students with real-world applications of business practices within the graphic design industry, contracts, the law, business planning, strategy and marketing. Students will assemble a strategic approach to estimating and planning, project workflow in a design business environment and create original work for service-learning client.
Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401,2100,2400,2401,3400,3403,3600.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1100and1102.
