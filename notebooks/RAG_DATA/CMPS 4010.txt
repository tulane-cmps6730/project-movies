CMPS 4010Capstone Project I(2)This is the first semester of a two-semester course devoted to the development of the student's capstone project, a required component of the Computer Science coordinate major.  Under supervision of a faculty advisor in computer science, students use the tools of computer science to solve a problem from another discipline, usually their primary major area.
