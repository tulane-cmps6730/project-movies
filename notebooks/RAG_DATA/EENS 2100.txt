EENS 2100Dynamic Planets(4)This course will explore the physical processes that operate within Earth, affecting how the geosphere interfaces with the biosphere, hydrosphere, cryosphere, and atmosphere. We will examine different processes, like volcanism, and forces, like gravity, and how affect the Earth and its inhabitants. Additionally, we will cover topics related to alternative energy resources, earthquakes, water resources and magnetism.Prerequisite(s):(MATH 1210*or1310*) or (MATH 1150*and1160*).*May be taken concurrently.
