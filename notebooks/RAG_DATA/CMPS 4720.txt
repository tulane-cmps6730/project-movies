CMPS 4720Machine Learning(3)This course will cover fundamental and advanced topics in machine learning. Topics will include linear and logistic regression, Lasso, preceptrons, deep neural networks, support vector machines, kernel methods, graphical models, principal and independent component analysis and Gaussian processes. In addition to thoroughly addressing theoretical aspects, several examples will illustrate the application of the different techniques.
Prerequisite(s): (CMPS 2170orMATH 2170) andCMPS 2200.Prerequisite(s):(CMPS 2170orMATH 2170) andCMPS 2200.
