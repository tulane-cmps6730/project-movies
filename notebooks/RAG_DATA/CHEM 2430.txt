CHEM 2430Organic Chemistry I: Deep-learning(3)An advanced introduction to organic chemistry and organic reaction mechanisms. This small class is focused on group-learning, peer-instruction, and problem solving. Evaluations will be based on non:standard testing, e.g., open-book and/or (effectively) open-ended testing. Three hours of lecture per week.Prerequisite(s):CHEM 1080and1085.Corequisite(s):CHEM 2435.
