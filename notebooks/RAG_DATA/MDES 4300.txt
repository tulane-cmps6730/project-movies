MDES 4300DMMC Studio(3)This course will provide a hands-on, applied learning experience using digital media and marketing communications automation software suites. Students will work in groups with real-life companies and organizations to create, implement and analyze the effectiveness of their digital media and marketing communication campaign. Peers, faculty, and other industry experts will evaluate and judge the performance of the group’s plan and results.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1100,2103,MDES 2300,2110,2210,3300,3450,3310and3320.
