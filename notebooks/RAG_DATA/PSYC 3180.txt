PSYC 3180Psychological Testing(3)PSYC 3180provides an overview of the process of psychological test development and analysis. After completing this course, students will be able to identify and describe the various types of psychological tests available for commercial use (e.g., intelligence tests, objective personality tests, projective personality techniques, neuropsychological tests, career interest inventories) and the criteria used to evaluate them.Prerequisite(s):PSYC 3090.
