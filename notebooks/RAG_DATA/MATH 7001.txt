MATH 7001Math Teaching Training(1)An interactive seminar to prepare students to teach mathematics at the college level. Topics covered will include discussion of practical issues such as how to keep a class engaged, how to prepare and grade assessments, how to prepare for class meetings, as well as discussion of theoretical issues such as what a teacher should be trying to achieve in the classroom, current understanding of how students learn best, and evaluating the effectiveness of assessments of student performance.
