EBIO 5970Capstone Research Seminars(3)EBIO5970/5971 is the capstone experience for majors in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology and Environmental Biology. A working assumption of our capstone is that you will go on to use your biological training in a professional capacity. Therefore, the course is designed to synthesize the major experience and prepare you for future career opportunities. You will engage in discussions of contemporary issues in EBIO and hone your skills in critical thinking and communication. EBIO5970 is the writing intensive version of the course. EBIO5971 students do not complete a paper.
