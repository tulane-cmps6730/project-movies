EDUC 3802Reading Methods Secondary Ed(3)This course addresses evidence-based reading research while studying the effective teaching of content area literacy.  An emphasis will be placed on developing adaptations of well-known strategies in addressing disciplinary ways of thinking, reading, and writing in the content areas.
