ECON 6330Intl Trading Relations(3)An examination of the principles of international trade and the international arrangements that have been established to guide international trade. Specific topics include comparative advantage, the effects of tariffs and quotas. Master's-level version ofECON 4330. Students should consult with Professor for additional assignments & different grading criteria.
