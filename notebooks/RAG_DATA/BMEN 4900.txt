BMEN 4900Art of Professional Eng(1)Research and Professional Practice (RPP) is a 2-semester sequence beginning in Spring of the Junior year.  It satisfies the University's "Writing Intensive" requirement.  A lecture series in the Spring semester, called "Art of Professional Engineering" includes economic analysis, ethics, professional communication including writing and oral presentation, research techniques including literature searching, citation, and the structure of a scientific paper.  Students must also register for either 4901 or 4902 in the Spring semester, and continue the sequence with 4911 or 4912/4930 in the following Fall semester.
