NSCI 3325Neuroanatomy Lab(1)The subject of this course is the anatomy of the human nervous system. Students will learn to identify and map the structure and position of nuclei, pathways, and anatomical divisions of the brain and spinal cord. The course is a practical correlate to Systems Neuroscience (NSCI 3320), and is intended to prepare students for further study in the neurosciences.
Prerequisite(s):CELL 3320* orNSCI 3320*.
