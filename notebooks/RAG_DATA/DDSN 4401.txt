DDSN 4401Design for Good(3)This studio course explores the greater purpose of design as an agent of change. Students will create multiple projects supporting a cause with a specific call to action. 2D, 3D and interactive elements will be employed to create meaningful works based on a concept. Students will work for personal and service-learning clients and communicate projects via professional written and verbal presentation.  Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401,2100,2400,2401,3400,3403,3600.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,2000and1401.
