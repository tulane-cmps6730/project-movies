HACR 1010Elementary Haitian Creole I(4)Designed for students with little or no knowledge of Haitian Creole, this class introduces students to the language and the culture of the creole speech community of the francophone Caribbean region, with emphasis on the Haitian speech community.  Students will acquire competencies in Haitian Creole that prepare them for more advanced studies in Haitian language and culture.  The course emphasizes the development of multiple language skills (listening, speaking, reading, writing, as well as interacting).
