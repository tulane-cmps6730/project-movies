CSMT 7920Software and Web App Security(3)The goal of this course is to provide cybersecurity managers and practitioners with an in-depth review of the technologies, models, best implementation practices and known software and web application vulnerabillities.  The course reviews activities and processes pertaining to the planning, programming, and management of software (and systems that manage software) including ways to secure applications through design and control interfaces.  This includes in-depth reviews of application architecture, trust models, key management, web protocols, and digital certificates.  This course also delves into means and measures to assess effectiveness of application security.
