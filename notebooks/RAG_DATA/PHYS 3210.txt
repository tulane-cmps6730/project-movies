PHYS 3210Molec Biophysics & Polymer Phy(3)An introduction to the physics of polymers and the physical bases underlying the biofunctionality of macromolecules in living systems. Themes of molecular self-organization, conformation, complementarity, and information content are emphasized and related to protein, lipid, and nucleic acid structure and processes. Introduction to scattering and other spectroscopic techniques.
Prerequisites:PHYS 2350or equivalent,CHEM 1070or equivalent, andMATH 1220or equivalent.Prerequisite(s):MATH 1220or1310.
