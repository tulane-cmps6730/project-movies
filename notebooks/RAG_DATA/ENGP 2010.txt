ENGP 2010Electric Circuits(3)A fundamental course dealing with electric charge, current, voltage, power, energy, and passive and active circuit elements. Response of linear circuits to steady state and time dependent signals, differential equations, circuit laws, network analysis, frequency response, phasors, and transfer functions. Prerequisites:MATH 1220andPHYS 1320.Prerequisite(s):PHYS 1320and (MATH 1220or1310).Corequisite(s):ENGP 2011.
