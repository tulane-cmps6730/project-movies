EDUC 3801Early Childhood Reading Methods(3)This course addresses evidence-based approaches for the effective teaching of literacy in PreK-3rd grade. The five essential components of reading instruction, as identified by the National Reading Panel, of this class include: phonemic awareness skills, phonics skills, reading fluency, vocabulary development, and comprehension strategies. The teacher candidate observes and learns to create rich literate environments which foster reading, writing, speaking, listening, and technological literacies through the integration of their foundation knowledge, use of instructional practices, approaches and methods, curriculum materials, and the appropriate use of assessment.Prerequisite(s):EDUCÂ 3420.
