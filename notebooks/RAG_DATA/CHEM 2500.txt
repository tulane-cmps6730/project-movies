CHEM 2500Environmental Chemistry(3)An overview of the many aspects of environmental chemistry. Topics include: aquatic chemistry, including water pollution and water treatment; atmospheric chemistry, air pollution and major threats to the global atmosphere; geochemistry and soil chemistry; nature, sources, and environmental chemistry of hazardous wastes; and toxicology chemistry.
Prerequisite(s):CHEM 1070and1080.Prerequisite(s):CHEM 1070and1080.
