BSLS 3320Compensation & Benefits(3)Students analyze the purpose, structure, and effectiveness of compensation systems; identify governing laws and resulting legal issues; examine methods of job design, analysis, and evaluation; review pay systems and the incentives and psychological and motivational aspects of pay, executive compensation, and compensation and benefits plan administration.   This course will not satisfy requirements toward majors in the School of Liberal Arts or the A.B. Freeman School of Business.
