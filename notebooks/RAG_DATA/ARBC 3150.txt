ARBC 3150Advanced Arabic(3)A continuation of skills developed inARBC 2030, which is a prerequisite forARBC 3150.  The Arabic language is used as the medium of instruction, and in addition to the further development of reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills, a greater emphasis is placed on culture. Prerequisite(s): minimum score of PASS in 'ARBC 3150Placement' orARBC 2030.Prerequisite(s):minimum score of PASS in 'ARBC 3150Placement' orARBC 2030.
