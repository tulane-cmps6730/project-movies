DDSN 4500Portfolio Studio(3)This studio course pairs students with expert faculty to further develop their portfolio for successful job placement upon graduation. This course may be taken twice for credit. Students will gain greater in-depth portfolio ready works, critique and evaluate works within dossier for improvement and apply greater focus to area of expertise. Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1500,1501,1502,1503,1504,1505,2500,2501,2502,2503,2504,2505,3500,3502,3503,3504.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 3503and3504.
