MGMT 7001Entrepreneurial Hosp Sem(3)This intensive seminar takes a deep dive into entrepreneurial hospitality and examines how hospitality is driving customer experience in a wide variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, financial services, and education. Companies are increasingly focused on hospitality as a mechanism to create market differentiation, build customer value, and provide a sustainable competitive advantage. This team-taught course provides a hands-on opportunity to learn from experts, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders.
