LGST 4170Employment Law(1-3)LGST 4170examines legal issues associated with the hiring process, such as recruitment; background checks; eligibility; hiring and promoting; and managing a diverse workforce, including affirmative action, harassment and accommodations. The course also covers conditions of employment such as pay, benefits, terms of employment, and so forth; managing performance; and terminating an employee, including terminating union, non-union and public sector employees.
Prerequisite(s):LGST 3010.Prerequisite(s):LGST 3010.
