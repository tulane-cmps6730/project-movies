MGMT 4150Environment, Society, and Capitalism(3)This course takes a strategic planning perspective to investigate environmental management issues in the context of assessing and responding to competitive and social forces. This course examines a serious challenge to corporations competing in the global economy: How to maximize profitability and production in such a way that will allow the planet to support operations indefinitely. Emphasis will be on the company's ability to use both traditional management concepts and new sustainability practices to build and sustain a competitive advantage. Students will learn how an enterprise can meet sustainability goals while still fulfilling its financial and market objectives. Junior Standing or Above.Prerequisite(s):ACCN 3010,FINE 3010,LGST 3010,MCOM 3010,MGMT 3010,MGSC 3010andMKTG 3010.
