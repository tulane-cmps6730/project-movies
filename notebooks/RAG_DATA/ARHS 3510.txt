ARHS 3510Rococo To Romanticism(3)In this course we will explore art produced in Europe from the early18th century through the mid-19th century.  We will consider the work, careers, and reputations of key artists such as Fragonard, David, Friedrich, Turner, Ingres, and Delacroix, among others, situating their work in relation to the political, socio-economic, and intellectual developments of the period.
