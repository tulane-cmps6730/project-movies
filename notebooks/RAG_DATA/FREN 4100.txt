FREN 4100French In Louisiana(3)An introduction to the French-related language varieties spoken in Louisiana: Cajun, Creole, and Colonial French. Examines the history of their implantation and development in Louisiana, their basic structural features, and the main sociolinguistic issues surrounding their use. Attention will also be given to language planning measures currently being taken to revitalize the French language in the state.
Prerequisite(s):FREN 3210.Prerequisite(s):FREN 3210.
