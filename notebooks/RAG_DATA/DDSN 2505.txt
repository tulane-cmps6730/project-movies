DDSN 2505Game Engine Studio II(3)This studio course examines the Unreal Engine for world-building, lighting and post-processing for a fully rendered scene. Students will create world building, incorporating lighting and post-processing, to assemble environment projects within a world for a fully-rendered scene within a real time environment. Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1500,1501,1502,1503,1504,1505,2500,2501,2502.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 2500,2501and2502.
