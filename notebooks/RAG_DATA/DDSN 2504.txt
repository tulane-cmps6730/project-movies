DDSN 2504Game Engine Studio I(3)This studio course explores the Unreal Engine interface and systems with an introduction of Blueprint scripting language. Students are expected to understand and apply Unreal Engine interface for world building, interface and navigating systems and premade assets. Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1500,1501,1502,1503,1504,1505,2500,2501,2502.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 2500,2501and2502.
