EBIO 2071Molecular and Evolutionary Genetics Recitation(0)This course is a required accompaniment toEBIO 2070-01 (Molecular and Evolutionary Genetics). Through readings, discussions, interactive exercises, and assignments, students will discuss the concepts and principles of genetics in an applied way, i.e. to apply genetics information to solving crosses and problems.
