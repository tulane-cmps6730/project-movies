SCEN 3035Anatomy & Physiology I Lab(1)The first of two sequenced laboratory courses that complementsSCEN 3030. Discussion of anatomical nomenclature, skeletal, muscular, peripheral and central nervous systems dissections. Dissection and exploration of human cadavers are an integral component of the laboratory experience.
Prerequisite(s):SCEN 3030*.
