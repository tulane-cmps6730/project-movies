Small Business Development Certificate Overview Requirements The 18-credit Professional Certificate Small Business Development provides students with the analytical skills and practical knowledge to develop business plans, create marketing strategies, identify capital resources, manage entrepreneurial ventures, and overcome the specific challenges that small businesses encounter.
