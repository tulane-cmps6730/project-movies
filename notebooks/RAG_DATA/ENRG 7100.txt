ENRG 7100Energy Mrkts, Inst & Pol(3)This course covers a range of energy-related topics including major challenges and policy issues facing the industry, history and structure of the industry, company profiles and strategies, energy economics, energy markets, energy regulation, energy technology, and sustainable development. Faculty associated with the Tulane Energy Institute will lecture on the history, structure, and economics of the energy sector and its importance in the growth of modern economies. The course also includes a series of presentations by industry participants including energy economists, sell-side analysts, industry regulators, upstream oil and gas operators, midstream and downstream participants, as well as representatives of the myriad companies that provide services to the direct participants.
