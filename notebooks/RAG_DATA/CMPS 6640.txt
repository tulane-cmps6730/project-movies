CMPS 6640Adv. Computational Geometry(3)This course focuses on advanced principles for designing and analyzing geometric algorithms and data structures, and their application to other disciplines. Selected topics may include: Dynamic and kinetic data structures, geometric algorithms and data structures in two and higher dimensions, shape analysis and matching, robustness and implementation issues, geometric approximation algorithms. Prerequisites: Introduction to Algorithms or equivalent, or permission by the instructor.CMPS 3130/6130 preferred.Prerequisite(s):CMPS 2200.
