GREK 2030Attic Prose(4)This course focuses on reading selections from a range of prose authors, e.g., Plato, Herodotus, Xenophon. In addition to improving their proficiency in reading Greek, students develop further familiarity with prose styles and begin to acquire skills in literary, historical, or philosophical analysis. Prerequisite:GREK 1020or equivalent.Prerequisite(s):minimum score of PASS in 'GREK 2030Placement' orGREK 1020.
