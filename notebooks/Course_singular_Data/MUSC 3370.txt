MUSC 3370Studies in Great Composers(3)The music of three influential composers is studied in depth against the background of their careers and times. The composers selected will change each term; contact the instructor or department for more information. Student must have ability to read, analyze, and discuss musical notation in order to be successful in the course.
Prerequisite(s):MUSC 1410*,1420* and2010*.
