ASLS 3050Introduction to Interpreting ASL to English to ASL(3)This course offers an overview of the history, philosophy, and theory of interpreting, specifically focusing on American Sign Language to English to American Sign Language. Students will discuss the mandate for equal access required by the ADA as well as agencies' application. We will learn about the market needs, the population served, the schema where interpreting takes place, certifications required, ethical requirements and testing systems. Prerequisite:ASLS 1010or equivalent.Prerequisite(s):ASLS 1010or minimum score of PASS in 'ASLS 3050Placement'.
