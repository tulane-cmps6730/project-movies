GLSP 4080Criminal Law(3)Through lectures and practical exercises, students explore the practice of criminal law, including criminal procedure and court rules, prosecutorial functions, probation, juvenile courts, mental commitment procedures, bail, and sentencing; and develop the skills necessary to perform investigations, discovery, alternative dispositions, and trial preparation.
Pre-requisites:GLSP 3030,3060and3070, or approval of Director.Prerequisite(s):GLSP 3030,3060and3070.
