PSYC 4330Neurobiol Learn & Memory(3)An introduction to the study of the neural mechanisms involved in learning and memory. The course will involve detailed study of the memory systems of the brain as well as historical trends, theoretical perspectives and empirical findings that are associated with the neurobiology of learning and memory. 
Prerequisite(s):PSYC 3300orNSCI 3300.Prerequisite(s):PSYC 3300orNSCI 3300.
