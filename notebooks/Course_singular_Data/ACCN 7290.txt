ACCN 7290Accounting Analytics(3)This hands-on course exposes students to all aspects of the data analytics process, including issue identification, data preparation, data analysis, and the communication of results. The primary objective of this course is to integrate knowledge of accounting, business, and data analytics to provide evidence to help solve business problems. Students will improve their ability to communicate information clearly and persuasively using written, verbal, and visual communication strategies. Students will also develop and expand skills in computer-related technologies, using mostly accounting-based datasets, which support the analysis of the problem as well as the communication of the results.
