MATH 3200Combinatorics(3)Basics of combinatorics with emphasis on problem solving. Provability, pigeonhole principle, mathematical induction. Counting techniques, generating functions, recurrence relations, Polya's counting formula, a theorem of Ramsey.
Prerequisite(s): (MATH 1210) and (MATH 1220) and (MATH 2210) or (MATH 3090) or (MATH 1310).Prerequisite(s):(MATH 1210) and (MATH 1220) and (MATH 2210) or (MATH 3090) or (MATH 1310).
