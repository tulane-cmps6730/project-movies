DDSN 2501Animation Studio II(3)This studio course examines rigging and weighting of characters within the animation interface. Scripting language is introduced. Students are expected to apply animation techniques to construct complex character rigs and techniques for advanced rigging solutions, skeleton, skinning associations with geometry.  Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1500,1501,1502,1503,1504,1505.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1504and1505.
