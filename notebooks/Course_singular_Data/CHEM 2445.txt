CHEM 2445Organic Chemistry Laboratory II: Deep-learning(1)An advanced laboratory to accompanyCHEM 2440(and therefore limited to 30 students). A continuation ofCHEM 2435. The laboratory continues to introduce organic synthesis techniques with a range of illustrative chemical syntheses combined with accompanying chromatographic, and Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) and Infrared (IR) Spectroscopic characterization of reaction components and products.Prerequisite(s):(CHEM 2410and2415) or (CHEM 2430and2435).Corequisite(s):CHEM 2440.
