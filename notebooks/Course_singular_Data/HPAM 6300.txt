HPAM 6300Data Visualization and Communication(3)Health organizations increasingly rely on data-driven decisions, requiring students to analyze and communicate pertinent information to inform stakeholders.HPAM 6300is an applied analytics course that focuses on data-centered communication by emphasizing best practices in data visualization and storytelling.  Students will build on the analytical skills developed inHPAM 6200by creating key performance indicators and generating dashboards to monitor performance.  Students will apply these skills by using cases and data visualization software, particularly Microsoft Excel and Tableau.Prerequisite(s):HPAM 6200.
