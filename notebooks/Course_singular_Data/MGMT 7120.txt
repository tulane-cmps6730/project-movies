MGMT 7120Competition & Strategy(3)Analytical tools are presented for formulating competitive strategies. In-depth analysis of several industries and competitors is undertaken to help predict competitors’ behavior and future industry evolution. Additional considerations include how government, technology, and other environmental factors affect competition. This course also provides analytical approaches to examine the corporate strategies of diversified firms. The principal focus will be on high technology industries and services.
