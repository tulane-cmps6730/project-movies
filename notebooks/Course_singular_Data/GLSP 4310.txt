GLSP 4310Employment Law(3)Through lectures and in-class discussions, students identify and break down the state and federal laws governing employment in the U.S., focusing upon the disputes that may arise in the workplace.  Practical exercises guide students in identifying and applying best practices for establishing, maintaining, and terminating the employment relationship; evaluating employment claims; and adhering to ethical practices. 
Pre-requisites:GLSP 3030,3060and3070, or approval of Director.  
Cross-listed withBSLS 3390– Employment & Labor Law (asynchronous online).Prerequisite(s):GLSP 3030,3060and3070.
