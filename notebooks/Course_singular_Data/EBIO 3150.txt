EBIO 3150Intro to GIS(4)This course is designed to give students a general understanding of geographic information systems (GIS) and the Environmental Systems Research Institute (ESRI) ArcGIS software. The approach taken is detailed instruction in utilizing ArcGIS to solve problems in the earth and environmental sciences. (SAME ASEBIO 6150,EENS 3150,EENS 6150,)
Corequisite(s):EBIO 3151.Corequisite(s):EBIO 3151.
