ECON 3710Economics of Education(3)An introduction to theory and evidence regarding economics of elementary, secondary, and higher education, Topics may include the economic returns to education for students, signaling versus human capital explanations, the rationale for government subsidies and regulation, accountability performance incentives for educators, and the rising cost of schools and colleges. Course may be repeated 1 time for credit.
