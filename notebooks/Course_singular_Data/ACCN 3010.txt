ACCN 3010Managerial Accounting(3)Course examines how managers use accounting information to formulate plans, control operations and make informed managerial decisions. Students will learn cost behavior and how to use cost information in managerial decisions. Students will also learn how to develop a corporate budget and gather feedback to ensure that the budget is either properly executed or adjusted.Prerequisite(s):ACCN 2010andINFO 1010.
