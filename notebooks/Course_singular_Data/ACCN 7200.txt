ACCN 7200Accounting Research(3)This course teaches academic research skills in the accounting arena. It applies economics, finance theory, and quantitative methods to study the use of accounting information. Major topics include the evolution of accounting and finance, fundamental analysis, and practical applications of accounting analysis.
