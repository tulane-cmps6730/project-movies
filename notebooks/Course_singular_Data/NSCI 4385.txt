NSCI 4385Cognitive Neuroscience Lab(1)A laboratory course that provides training in experimental design and ethical issues, data collection, analysis, and manuscript preparation for cognitive neuroscience experiments. Methods used in cognitive neuroscience research, such as event-related potentials, structural and functional MRI, also will be discussed. Students will conduct their own studies using behavioral and brain electrical activity measures. Note: Satisfies psychology and neuroscience laboratory requirement. Fulfills college laboratory requirement.
Prerequisite(s): (NSCI 3300, 3670,PSYC 3300or 3670) and NSCI 380*.
