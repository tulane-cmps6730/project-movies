DDSN 4402Packaging Design(3)This studio course uses good design techniques paired with visual marketing and advertising strategies to apply design to three-dimensional packaging objects. Students will work alone and in groups to create concept-driven solutions to appeal to an audience via packaging media. Students will communicate projects via professional written and verbal presentation.
Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401,2100,2400,2401,3400,3403,3600.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401and2000.
