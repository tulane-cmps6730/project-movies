DDSN 4405Design Studio III(3)This studio course explores development of multi-media works surrounding project themes. Students will use print (2D), packaging (3D) and interactive media to communicate a project solution. Students will work alone and in groups to create original works for mock clients and service-learning experiences. Students will create professional written and verbal presentations for portfolio-ready printed works.
Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401,2100,2400,2401,3400,3403,3600,4400,4402,4404.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401,2401,3400,2400and2000.
