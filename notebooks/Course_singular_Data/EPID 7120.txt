EPID 7120Epidemiologic Methods II(3)This course is intended to enhance student understanding of observational epidemiologic research methods.  The course emphasizes critical thinking and approaches to design, analysis and interpretation of observational studies. Emphasis is placed on maximizing study internal validity. Selected topics covered include measures of disease frequency, association, and impact; study design options, sources of bias, and data analysis techniques.Prerequisite(s):SPHLÂ 6060.
