CLIN 5120Immigration Law Clinic(3)Students in the Immigrants' Rights Law Clinic represent detainees, migrant workers, children and other immigrants with critical legal needs working through the U.S. Immigration system. Working alongside licensed attorneys, students work on behalf of clients and community groups in a variety of settings--immigration agencies and courts, state and federal courts, as well as work-shops in detention centers and/or community centers.Course Limit:2
