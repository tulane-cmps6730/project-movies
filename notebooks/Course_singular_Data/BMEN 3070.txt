BMEN 3070Quantitative Physiology(3)This course places emphasis upon the chemical basis of life; cells and cellular metabolism; histology and tissues; the endocrine, skeletal and nervous systems; respiratory, digestive, cardiovascular, lymphatic and reproductive systems; nutrition and metabolism; water, electrolyte and acid-base balance, and human growth and development.Prerequisite(s):CHEM 1070,1080,CELL 1010andBMEN 2600.Corequisite(s):BMEN 3075.
