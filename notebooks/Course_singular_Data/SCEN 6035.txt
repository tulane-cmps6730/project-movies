SCEN 6035Anatomy & Physiology I Lab(1)The first of two sequenced laboratory courses that complementsSCEN 6030. Discussion of anatomical nomenclature, skeletal, muscular, peripheral and central nervous systems dissections. Dissection and exploration of human cadavers are an integral component of the laboratory experience. (Graduate section ofSCEN 3035.) 
Prerequisite(s):SCEN 6030*.
