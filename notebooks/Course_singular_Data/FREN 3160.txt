FREN 3160Advanced French Through Media II(3)This course is a continuation of the objectives set inFREN 3150: it is designed to reinforce communicative skills in French at the advanced level through exposure to authentic written, visual, and oral documents dealing with French and Francophone cultures. The course offers a thorough review of French grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation, while further developing students' media analysis skills.
Prerequisite(s):FREN 3150or minimum score of PASS in 'FREN 3160Placement'.FREN 3150may be taken concurrently.Prerequisite(s):FREN 3150*or minimum score of PASS in 'FREN 3160Placement'.*May be taken concurrently.
