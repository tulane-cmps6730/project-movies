CMPS 4250Math Found Comp Security(3)This course studies the mathematics underlying computer security, including both public key and symmetric key cryptography, crypto-protocols and information flow. The course includes a study of the RSA encryption scheme, stream and clock ciphers, digital signatures and authentication. It also considers semantic security and analysis of secure information flow.
Prerequisite(s): (MATH 1160or1220) and (MATH 2170orCMPS 2170).Prerequisite(s):(MATH 1220or1310) and (MATH 2170orCMPS 2170).
