EBIO 3290Behavioral Ecology(3)This course addresses the ecological and evolutionary causes and consequences of animal behavior, using both proximate and ultimate approaches.  Topics include sociality, mating systems, sexual selection, animal movement, signals, behavior and conservation, and cognition.
