CHEM 3410Macromolecular, Supramolecular, and Nanochemistry(3)An advanced courses for those interested in chemistry of nanomaterials and polymers.  This should be beyond the two courses of general chemistry and the two courses of organic chemistry.  Evaluations will be based on quizzes, tests, plus the final exam.  The class will be schedule three hours per week.Prerequisite(s):(CHEM 2420and2425) or (CHEM 2440and2445).
