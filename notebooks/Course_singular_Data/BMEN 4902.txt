BMEN 4902SR Research Prof Experience I(2)This two-course sequence is designed to facilitate an individual biomedical research or design experience in a laboratory.  Students will be intorduced to the tools, techniques, and rules necessary to function independently and professionally as a researcher or engineer.  Topics include thesis writing, technical communication, and time management.  The main component of the course is a two semester long research or design project under the direction of a faculty member, scientist or other professional.
