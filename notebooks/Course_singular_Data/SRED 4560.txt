SRED 4560Real Estate Internship(1-3)Open to upper level students in good standing, the Real Estate Internship course provides students with an opportunity to reinforce and expand their skills and knowledge through professional internship experiences during the course of their studies.  Students must consult with the Real Estate Major Advisor to get approval for selected work assignment prior to registration. The course may be repeated for up to 6 credit hours but only 3 credit hours may count towards general Real Estate elective credit.Course Limit:2Maximum Hours:6
