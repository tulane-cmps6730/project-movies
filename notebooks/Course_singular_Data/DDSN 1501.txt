DDSN 1501Digital Anatomy Game Art & Ani(3)This studio course explores traditional fine art drawing paired with digital drawing techniques. Students will apply understanding of anatomy to character development for games and animation. Students will analyze landscape proportions, surface features of human figure and digital anatomic features for image making. Students will then apply digital Zbrush techniques to achieve rendering outcomes. 
Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1500.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1500.
