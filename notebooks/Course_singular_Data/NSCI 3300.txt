NSCI 3300Brain and Behavior(3)Lectures cover the function and structure of the nervous system and the role of brain activity in the regulation of behavior. This course provides Neuroscience majors with a first exposure to the biological bases of behavior and should be taken prior to other Neuroscience courses at the 3000-level and above.
Prerequisite(s):PSYC 1000.Prerequisite(s):PSYC 1000.
