EPID 6600Social Epidemiology: Mechanisms of Disparities(3)This course will examine the major patterning of health related to social factors, including race, gender, and socioeconomic status. Effects from the microsystem (individual experience, family) to the macrosystem (neighborhood, community, nation) will be examined.
