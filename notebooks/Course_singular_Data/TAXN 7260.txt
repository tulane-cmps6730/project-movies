TAXN 7260Taxation of Individuals(3)The federal system of taxation, as it relates to individuals, is examined. The course uses a problem-solving approach, wherein students analyze the facts presented and synthesize rules and concepts in arriving at a solution to individual tax problems. The course is "Code" (Internal Revenue Code) oriented, emphasizing the primary authorities that govern tax matters.
