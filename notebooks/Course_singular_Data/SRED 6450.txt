SRED 6450Sustainable Real Estate Development Internship(3)Tulane's MSRED program offers our graduate students paid internships with local organizations involved in real estate development. These GIGs (“Graduate Internship…GO!”) are semester-long positions for approximately 15 hours/week. The primary goals of this program are to supplement the MSRED program’s academic work with practical experience and to build confidence and professional networks for our students.
