EBIO 4300Biology of Amphibians and Reptiles(4)This course will provide an introduction to herpetology, the study of reptiles and amphibians. Topics covered will include the evolutionary history, systematics, physiology, ecology, life history, behavior and conservation of amphibians and reptiles. The course consists of two lectures and a lab or field trip each week. Occasional weekend field trips may also be scheduled.
Prerequisite(s):EBIO 1010,1015andCELL 1010.
Corequisite(s):EBIO 4301.Prerequisite(s):EBIO 1010,1015andCELL 1010.Corequisite(s):EBIO 4301.
