CMPS 4020Capstone Project II(2)This is the second of a two-semester course devoted to the development of the student’s capstone project, a required component of  the Computer  Science coordinate major. Under supervision of a faculty advisor in computer science, students use the tools of computer science to solve a problem from another discipline, usually their primary major area.
