EBIO 6030Field Botany(4)A plant identification course focusing on terrestrial and aquatic flora of Louisiana. Lab field trips include visits to a variety of local ecosystems from coastal wetlands to upland forests. Lectures cover botany, taxonomy, and the ecological and evolutionary processes that structure plant communities, with special emphasis on how these topics apply to Louisiana ecosystems.
Corequisite(s):EBIO 6031.Corequisite(s):EBIO 6031.
