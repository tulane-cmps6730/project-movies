ENHS 6540Occupational Health(3)This course provides a foundation in occupational health for physicians and professionals working in health and safety programs.  The course provides an overview of:  regulatory issues in occupational health; occupational health services, systems and policy; occupational toxicology and biological monitoring; occupational diseases including pulmonary dermatology; hearing conservation and hearing loss; workers compensation and return to work; and ergonomics. The course uses and interactive case study approach.
