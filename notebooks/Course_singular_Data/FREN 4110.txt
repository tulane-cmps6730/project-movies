FREN 4110Field Research on French in Louisiana(3)Students will interview and record speakers of Cajun, Creole, and Colonial French in various parts of Louisiana. Working individually and in groups, they will then transcribe the recordings for purposes of linguistic description and analysis.
Prerequisite(s):FREN 3140.Prerequisite(s):FREN 3140.Corequisite(s):FREN 4891.
