NSCI 3780Sex, Gender, and the Brain(3)How do we define sex?  How do we define gender?  How do we communicate research on sex differences with the public?  Using an inclusive framework, we will learn the basics of sexual development and differentiation, including prenatal, adolescent, and beyond.  Then, with a critical lens, we will explore current neuroscience research regarding gender/sex differences in humans and animal models.Prerequisite(s):(NSCI 3300orPSYC 3300).
