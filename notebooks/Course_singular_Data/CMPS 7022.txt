CMPS 7022Research in Computer Science(3)In this course PhD students engage in a research project in Computer Science, under the direction of a faculty member, normally the student's faculty advisor. At the beginning of the course the scope of the project will be determined by a project proposal prepared by the student. Research will be conducted on an individual basis with the faculty advisor. The student will prepare a final report that summarize the research methodologies and the research outcomes. The content of this course varies from semester to semester.
