BSLS 3380Business Ethics(3)Applying theoretical critique and case-oriented methods, students analyze the moral, ethical, and value issues that challenge business, industry, and corporate life, with a focus upon identifying the ethical principles and strategies that govern the management process and developing a personal business ethics code of conduct.  This course will not satisfy requirements toward majors in the School of Liberal Arts or the A.B. Freeman School of Business.
Note: Satisfies one Humanities course requirement for School of Professional Advancement majors.
