ECON 4540Public Finance & Public Policy(3-4)An overview of the field of public finance and public policy.Topics include the rationale for government intervention in the economy, the efficiency, distributional, and revenue effects of taxation, and the evaluation of specific public policies and tax instruments.ECON 6540is the masters level equivalent, open to graduate students only.  Prerequisite(s):ECON 3010or 3030.Prerequisite(s):ECON 3010or 3030.
