MATH 3090Linear Algebra(4)An introduction to linear algebra emphasizing matrices and their applications. Gaussian elimination, determinants, vector spaces and linear transformations, orthogonality and projections, eigenvector problems, diagonalizability, Spectral Theorem, quadratic forms, applications. MATLAB is used as a computational tool.Prerequisite(s):MATH 2210.Corequisite(s):MATH 3091.
