DDSN 2503Animation Studio III(3)This studio course explores advanced animation techniques as well as motion capture technology. Students will understand and apply motion capture system and integrate motion capture within traditional animation principles. Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1500,1501,1502,1503,1504,1505,2500,2501,2502.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 2500,2501and2502.
