CSMT 7750Wireless, Mobile & Cloud Secur(3)This course reviews architecture, vulnerabilities and security methods for Wireless, Mobile and Cloud Computing technologies in the enterprise.  Included topics will be reviewing these network constructs as well as vulnerabilities, and attack vectors.  Also covered are best practices for implementing and securing IEEE 802.11 wireless networks, wireless access and BYOD, wireless security protocols, mobile IP communications, and cloud computing categories and services.  This course also looks at current and emerging cloud services and cloud-based applications, including the "Internet of Things".
