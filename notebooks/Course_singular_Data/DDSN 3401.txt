DDSN 3401Letterpress Studio(3)This studio course explores traditional letterpress techniques within a design context. Students will use their original typographic compositions and translate them to the physical letterpress printing method. Students will critique and analyze design solutions via written assignments and verbal class discussions.
Prerequisites:DDSN 1100,1101,1102,1400,1401,2100,2400,2401.Prerequisite(s):DDSN 1100and1400.
