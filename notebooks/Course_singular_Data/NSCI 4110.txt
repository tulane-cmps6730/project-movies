NSCI 4110Brain and Language(3)The goal of this course is to learn how the brain is organized to produce and comprehend language and to understand linguistic disorders attendant on brain damage. There is an optional service learning component in which students can work with a speech therapist at a local health-care provider.
Prerequisite(s):NSCI 3300orPSYC 3300.Prerequisite(s):NSCI 3300orPSYC 3300.
