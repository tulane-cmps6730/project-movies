GESS 4500Gender and Archives(3)This course focuses on developing knowledge of major theories of archives, on fostering research skills by engaging with materials, and on involving students in archival work that will allow questions about gender. Exploring theories and practices of archives, the course takes students through the history of archives, with special attention to women as keeping, and represented in, scholarly collections.
Prerequisite(s):GESS 2900.Prerequisite(s):GESS 2900.
