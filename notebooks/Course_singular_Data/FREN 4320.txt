FREN 4320Renaissance Literature(3)A survey of representative works of outstanding authors of the period: Marot, Rabelais, Ronsard, Du Bellay, Montaigne, and D'Aubigné.  Both poetry and prose will be studied against the backdrop of the history and civilization of the Renaissance in France.
Prerequisite(s):FREN 3210.Prerequisite(s):FREN 3210.
