HPAM 6380Organizational Behavior(3)HPAM 6380introduces students to theories and concepts of organizational behavior within the context of healthcare.  Students will consider influences such as emotional intelligence, values, psychological safety, perceptions, motivation, power, persuasion, and conflict, and how they affect individuals, teams and organizations.HPAM 6380will consider current pressures and challenges in the current environment and how they affect culture, strategy and change management in healthcare organizations.
