ITAL 3130Advanced Convers & Comp(3)The course aims primarily at perfecting the student's speaking and writing ability. Articles taken from newspapers, periodicals, the Internet, etc., serve as a basis for discussion and familiarize students with contemporary Italy. The course presupposes a solid grammatical foundation and any grammar review is given only on an individual basis.
Prerequisite(s):ITAL 2030or minimum score of PASS in 'ITAL 3130Placement'.Prerequisite(s):ITAL 2030or minimum score of PASS in 'ITAL 3130Placement'.
