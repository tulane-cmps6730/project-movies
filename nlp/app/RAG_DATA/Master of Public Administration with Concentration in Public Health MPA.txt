Master of Public Administration with Concentration in Public Health, MPA Overview Requirements The School of Professional Advancement (SoPA) and the School of School of Public Health and Tropical Medicine (SPHTM) offer a 36 credit MPA with Public Health concentration. This option is provided for MPA students who seek deeper knowledge in Public Health. Upon completion of requirements, students will be awarded an MPA degree awarded by SoPA and a Public Health Concentration awarded by the SPHTM. Requirements for the MPA degree with Public Health concentration are detailed below.
