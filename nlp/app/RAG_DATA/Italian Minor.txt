Italian Minor Overview Requirements A minor in Italian consists of SIX courses or 18 credits above ITAL 2030 Intermediate Italian (4 c.h.) . A minor in Italian consists of SIX courses or 18 credits above ​ ITAL 2030 Intermediate Italian (4 c.h.) .The student is required to take the following courses including: Course List Course ID Title Credits ITAL 3130 Advanced Convers & Comp 3 ITAL 3250 Italian Lang & Culture 3 Four electives in literature or related field * 12 Total Credit Hours 18 Additional Information In order to take a 4000-level course taught in Italian a student must have successfully completed a 3000-level course taught in Italian or have received a 4000-level placement. Students are encouraged to participate in a study abroad program approved by the department. The student can count courses taken abroad towards the minor only with prior approval by the Italian Undergraduate Advisor. * Electives May include internships. May include one course taught in English upon consultation with the Italian Undergraduate Advisor.
