Dance, BA Overview Requirements The Bachelor of Arts Dance curriculum focuses on development of technical abilities, and choreographic skill, with dance related electives.  The program strives to unite academic and artistic inquiry with dance related courses and cross-over course work, as applicable, in other disciplines . Audition is required. Curriculum Requires a minimum of 35 credit hours. Note: Audition is required for the BA.  Auditions are held every year in the fall semester.
