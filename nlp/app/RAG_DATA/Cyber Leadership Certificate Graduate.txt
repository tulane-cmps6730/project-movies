Cyber Leadership Certificate (Graduate) Overview Requirements This Graduate Certificate is a twelve-credit course of study in which students will be exposed to cybersecurity leadership concepts related to the governance,  policy, and management of securing and protecting digital assets. All credits in this certificate may be applied to the Master of Science Graduate Degree in Cybersecurity Management.
